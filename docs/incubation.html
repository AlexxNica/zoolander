What is Incubation?

Elements currently in development and/or in active use but not officially supported by Zoolander.

FAQs

  • How does a component become officially supported?

    Via a long and complicated process which involves many people in the company. Kidding! Brand needs to approve it.

  • Can I use components in incubation?

    Yes! Brand doesn't have as high velocity as other teams. Just verify if the component is "Ready" or "In-Flight"
